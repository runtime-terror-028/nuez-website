Smart Benches

Smart benches are innovative pieces of urban furniture equipped with various technological features designed to enhance public spaces. These benches typically include solar panels for sustainable energy, USB charging ports for mobile devices, Wi-Fi hotspots for internet access, and environmental sensors to monitor air quality and weather conditions.
