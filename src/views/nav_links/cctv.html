IR Day/Night CCTV

IR cameras are equipped with infrared LEDs. These LEDs allow them to capture clear images in low-light conditions or complete darkness. They are particularly useful for nighttime surveillance.

Indoor CCTV

These cameras are designed for indoor use, such as homes, offices, and retail spaces. They are typically smaller and more discreet, making them suitable for inconspicuous surveillance. However, they may not be weatherproof.

Bullet Button

Bullet cameras have a cylindrical or bullet-like shape. They are often weatherproof and easy to mount on walls or ceilings. Due to their robust design, they are commonly used for outdoor surveillance.

Outdoor CCTV

Outdoor cameras are built to withstand harsh weather conditions. They are weatherproof and durable, making them ideal for monitoring exterior areas like entrances, parking lots, and gardens. Many outdoor cameras also feature night vision capabilities.

PTZ CCTV

PTZ cameras offer pan, tilt, and zoom functionalities. They can rotate horizontally (pan), tilt vertically (tilt), and zoom in or out. These cameras are controlled remotely and are excellent for covering large areas or tracking moving objects.
