Solar Panels

Smart benches often come with integrated solar panels to generate renewable energy, powering the bench's various features.

USB Charging Ports

Equipped with USB ports, allowing users to charge their mobile devices using the bench's solar-generated power.

Wireless Charging

Some smart benches offer wireless charging pads for compatible devices, providing a convenient charging solution without cables.

Wi-Fi Hotspot

Many smart benches provide Wi-Fi connectivity, acting as a hotspot for users to access the internet.

Environmental Sensors

These benches can include sensors to monitor environmental conditions such as air quality, temperature, humidity, and noise levels.
